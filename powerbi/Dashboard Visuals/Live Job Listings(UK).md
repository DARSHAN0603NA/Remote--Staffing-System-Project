# Live Job Listings (UK)

**Description**: 
This table provides a detailed view of live job opportunities across the United Kingdom. It displays individual job records with key attributes such as company name, job title, location, salary range, experience required, and employment type.

**What this visual shows:**
- Real-time job listings from multiple companies  
- Job roles and corresponding hiring organizations  
- Location-wise job availability within the UK  
- Salary ranges and required experience levels  
- Employment types such as full-time and temporary roles  

**Purpose:**
This visual enables recruiters, analysts, and stakeholders to explore granular job-level data in a structured format. It supports role-based filtering, salary comparison, and hiring trend analysis, making it useful for both operational review and strategic workforce planning.
