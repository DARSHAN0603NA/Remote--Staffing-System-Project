# Geographic Distribution of Jobs (UK)

<img width="912" height="530" alt="Geographical Distribution of Jobs" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/879aefb6-061d-4ec3-929e-f51e0c2d45f0" />


**Description**:  
This visual represents the geographic distribution of job opportunities across the United Kingdom using an interactive map. Each data point corresponds to job postings clustered by location, providing a clear spatial view of hiring demand.

**What this visual shows**:
- Concentration of job listings across different UK regions  
- High-demand hiring zones highlighted through dense clusters  
- Comparison of job availability between metropolitan and non-metropolitan areas  

**Purpose:**  
This map helps recruiters, analysts, and stakeholders quickly identify regional hiring trends, talent demand hotspots, and location-based workforce distribution. It supports location-driven decision-making for recruitment planning and market analysis.
